Our Mission

Written collectively by the SUNY Fredonia Spring '18 Literary Publishing class

The Trident aims to expose the fresh ways that language can be spun with excellence. Much like the three prongs of a trident, we strive for our issues to be inclusive, fair, and thought-provoking.

We believe all authors deserve and are entitled to an equal opportunity to highlight their work in a professional setting. We do our best to represent authors of multiple backgrounds.

All of the pieces included in our magazine are selected via blind-read submissions. The evaluation process is rigorous, but we are interested in variety, and all undergraduate submissions are given fair consideration.

We encourage students to share a piece of themselves, to showcase their hidden literary talents, and also be moved by the works of others. The Trident gives you the opportunity to express yourself, whether it be through a goofy story or a heartfelt poem.

We pride ourselves on giving new writers the opportunity to share their work and explore their artistic creativity. Here at the Trident, we know it is hard to get your work published, especially when you're fairly new to the game.

At the end of the day, we hope that many will be able to say, "It all started at the Trident."
